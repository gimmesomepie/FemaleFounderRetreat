About The Retreat


Created by a Former Corporate Woman, for Women Who Carry a Lot

Over 5 days, we combine yoga, Vipassana-inspired meditation, sound healing, Thai massage, and nature immersion to help you reset your nervous system, clear your mind, and reconnect with your inner clarity.


This retreat is designed specifically for female leaders who give so much of themselves to others — and are ready to finally give something back to themselves.

Daily Yoga

Vinyasa, Yin & Restorative sessions to balance strength and softness.

Vipassana Meditation

Mindfulness practices for nervous system balance and clarity.

Sound Healing

Therapeutic vibrations from singing bowls calm the nervous system and guide the body into a deep state of relaxation. The sound waves help quiet the mind, release emotional and physical tension, and create space for inner balance and renewal.

Healthy Meals

Our healthy meals are prepared by our Thai and Morrocan chef. Combining the two tastful cultures that have the same love for food.

Luxury Accommodation

Mountain boutique resort with pool, spa & nourishing meals.

Connect with other Female Founders and Leaders

The goal of this retreat is not only create a retreat space for the female leaders but also to connect Female leaders together. It is nothing more heartwarming to know that there are other female leaders who are going through the same struggles. We will share our experiences through leading and how each individual handling their day-to-day couping.
